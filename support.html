Common Questions

  • How do I add a family member? Tap the "+" icon in the Family Profile tab.
  • Why aren't my reminders working? Ensure notifications are enabled for MediSync in your iPhone's Settings.
  • Can I use MediSync offline? Yes, you can view your schedule, but syncing requires an active internet connection.
