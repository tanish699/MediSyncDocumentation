Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 18, 2026

1. Types of Data Collected

To provide our services, MediSync collects:

  • Personal Data: Email addresses (used for account creation/login).
  • Health & Medication Data: Medication details, schedules, inventory, and family member profiles entered by the user.
  • Device Info: Basic crash diagnostics (if enabled by Apple) to improve app stability.

2. How the Data is Used

We use your data solely to provide app functionality: syncing your medication schedules, sending you local reminders, and allowing you to track your family's adherence across your devices.

3. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell your data. We utilize a secure backend infrastructure provider (Supabase) to store and sync your data securely. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

4. Data Storage and Protection

Your data is stored securely in our cloud backend with restricted access. Users are required to authenticate via Apple/Email to access their customized profile, ensuring personal privacy.

5. User Rights

You have the right to access the data we hold about you or request complete deletion of your data and account. If you wish to delete your account, contact us at the email provided below.
